Facilities included in Premium Strength

STRENGTH AREA

Strength areas are the bedrock of our character, embodying the essence of our capabilities and resilience. They define our competence, where we excel effortlessly and shine brightest. Within these domains, we find our most potent tools for tackling challenges and seizing opportunities.

CARDIO AREA

Cardiovascular exercise, commonly known as cardio, is vital for maintaining a healthy heart and body. Engaging in activities like running, cycling, or dancing can improve circulation and lung capacity, enhancing overall cardiovascular health.

TURF ZONE AREA

The turf zone area offers a versatile space for a wide range of activities and exercises. It provides a cushioned surface ideal for high-impact workouts, such as plyometrics and agility drills, minimizing the risk of injury.

YOGA AREA

Revitalize your mind, body, and spirit in our state-of-the-art yoga area. Step into a space designed to inspire serenity and rejuvenation, where every breath becomes a journey inward. With our tranquil ambiance and expertly curated environment, discover the transformative power of yoga.

Step into luxury and comfort with our meticulously maintained bathrooms, where cleanliness meets tranquility. Immerse yourself in a sanctuary of pristine hygiene and modern elegance, designed to elevate your restroom experience. Our attention to detail ensures that every surface gleams with freshness, promising a refreshing retreat after your workout or relaxation session.

Refresh and rejuvenate in our pristine shower area, where relaxation meets cleanliness. Step into a haven of tranquility and luxury, designed to invigorate your senses and elevate your post-workout routine. Our spacious showers are meticulously maintained to ensure the highest standards of hygiene, providing you with a revitalizing experience every time.

Step into our locker room haven, where cleanliness meets convenience. Indulge in refreshing showers and secure storage for your belongings. From pre to post-workout, our facilities ensure a seamless transition, enhancing your fitness journey every step of the way.
