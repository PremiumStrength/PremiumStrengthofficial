BEST SHOULDER WORKOUT
Focusing on shoulder muscle workouts is key for strength and fitness maintenance

To Target Front Delt Shoulder

Shoulder Press

The shoulder press is an upper-body exercise targeting trapezoid, deltoids, and triceps, with primary and secondary muscles including the triceps, upper chest, traps, and core.

Front Raise

The front raise strengthens shoulder muscles and upper chest, enhancing shoulder flexion and enhancing shoulder definition, crucial for safe object lifting in daily life.

Landmine Press

The Landmine Press targets the entire upper body and core, focusing on the shoulders, chest, triceps, core, glutes, and upper back for stability and balance.

To Target Side Delt Shoulder

Dumbell Raise

The Dumbbell Front Raise is a weightlifting exercise that targets the anterior deltoids of the shoulders by raising dumbbells in front of the body.

Upright Rows

The upright row is a compound weight exercise that targets shoulder muscles, including deltoids, trapezius, rhomboids, biceps, and triceps, with a focus on deltoid muscles.

Cable Raise

The cable front raise targets the rounded shoulder contour muscles, pectoralis major, upper trapezius, and serratus anterior, while also working the upper fibers.

To Target Rear Delt Shoulder

Face Pull

Face pulls, a shoulder exercise targeting posterior deltoids, are popular among gym-goers and athletes due to their importance in training routines and their use of a cable pulley machine.

Rear Delt Fly

Rear delt fly is a resistance exercise that targets the posterior deltoid muscle at the back of the shoulder, increasing deltoid muscle definition and strength, particularly the posterior deltoids.

Pec Deck Fly

The Pec Deck Fly targets the pectoralis major, primarily the sternal head, but also works other chest muscles, shoulders, and arms.
