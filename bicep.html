BEST BICEP WORKOUT
Targeting our biceps through workouts enhances arm strength, function, and aesthetic appeal

To Target Long Head Muscle

Dumbell Curl

The dumbbell curl is a low-difficult, low-weight exercise targeting biceps, typically used as a finishing or accessory movement after heavier pull exercises.

Ez Bar Curl

This isolation exercise targets biceps using an EZ-Bar for added stability, allowing for more focus on exertion and strength development.

Spider Curl

Spider curls can enhance biceps size by targeting both long and short biceps brachii muscles, as well as triceps and brachialis, an essential elbow flexor muscle.

To Target Upper Back

Hammer Curl

Hammer curls target biceps heads, brachialis, and brachioradialis, enhancing bicep peak and defining arms. They strengthen grip for deadlifts, pulling, and strength movements.

Cable Hammer Curl

The exercise aims to enhance muscular endurance, work capacity, and strength by training with low-to-moderate loads and shorter rest times.

Reverse Hammer Curl

Reverse curls are an exercise that targets the brachioradialis and brachialis muscles, using an overhand grip to shift focus from the biceps to the forearm.
