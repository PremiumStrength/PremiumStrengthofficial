Workout Tips For Your Fitness Journey

(One workout per category)

Chest Workout

8-12 reps for muscle size (hypertrophy).
10-15 reps for a balance between strength and size.
15-20 reps for high-repetition workouts occasionally.

Shoulder Workout

Improving Strength: 1-6 reps and 3-6 sets
Building Muscle Size: 6-12 reps and 2-3 sets
Enhancing Endurance: 15+ reps and 1-2 sets

Tricep Workout

Target a rep range of 6-12 reps per set for optimal muscle growth.



Back Workout

3-4 sets of each back exercise using loads that are 70 to 85% of your 1RM(rep amx) for 8 to 12 reps.

Bicep Workout

3 sets of 10 reps for biceps exercises. This falls within the optimal rep range (8-12) linked to muscle growth

Leg Workout

Improving Strength: 1-6 reps and 3-6 sets
Building Muscle Size: 6-12 reps and 2-3 sets
Enhancing Endurance: 15+ reps and 1-2 sets
