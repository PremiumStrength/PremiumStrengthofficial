BEST BACK WORKOUT
Strengthening our back muscles promotes posture, stability, and reduces the risk of injuries

To Target Lats (Wings) back

Pull Ups

Pullups are upper body strength exercises targeting arm, shoulder, and back muscles, primarily targeting the latissimus dorsi, chest, upper back, and shoulder, while also stabilizing the abs.

Barbell Row

The barbell row enhances trunk strength, promotes spine stability, and targets multiple muscles simultaneously, enhancing daily life and enhancing the effectiveness of other exercises.

Straight Arms Pull Down

The straight-arm pulldown is beneficial for lifters who struggle with their lats, as it prevents mid-back and biceps from dominating the movement, allowing more direct focus.

To Target Upper Back

Closed Grip Cable Row

The close grip cable row exercise targets middle back muscles and latissimus dorsi, focusing on thickening the back instead of just examining its width.

Wide Grip Cable Row

The wide grip cable row exercise targets the back muscles, primarily the latissimus dorsi, using a wider grip on a straight bar or pull-down bar attachment.

T-Bar Row

The T-bar row is an intermediate-level compound exercise that targets the latissimus dorsi, a part of the back side of the torso.
