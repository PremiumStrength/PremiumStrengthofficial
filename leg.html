To Target Quad Muscles

Barbell Squat

Barbell squats are a compound exercise that targets quads, glutes, hamstrings, calves, abs, lower back muscles, and upper back muscles when performed correctly.

Hack Squat

The hack squat is a lower body exercise that targets the quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, and calves using a hack squat machine, offering a safer, more controlled variation than traditional free weights.

Leg Extension

Leg extensions are an isolation exercise that targets the quadriceps, the large muscles at the front of the thigh, by extending the lower legs against resistance.

To Target Hamstring Muscles

Leg Curl

Leg curl is a popular exercise in gyms or fitness centers that targets hamstrings in the back of the thigh, improving strength, size, and endurance.

Romanian Deadlift

The Romanian deadlift is a popular bodybuilding exercise that targets glutes, hamstrings, and core, enhancing posterior chain muscle strength and strengthening both core and lower body.

Single Leg Romanian Deadlift

The Romanian deadlift is a low-impact exercise that enhances hamstring and glute health, hip joint function, and hamstring engagement, thereby improving bilateral strength and powerformance.
